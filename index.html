About Mushroom Kingdom

Explore the enchanting world of the Mushroom Kingdom, home to Mario, Luigi, Princess Peach, and many other beloved characters. From the lush green hills of Yoshi's Island to the fiery depths of Bowser's Castle, there's something for every traveler to discover.

Popular Destinations

Toad Town

Visit Toad Town, the bustling capital of the Mushroom Kingdom. Explore its colorful streets, meet friendly Toads, and maybe even catch a glimpse of Princess Peach's castle.

Mount Beanpole

Climb Mount Beanpole for breathtaking views of the Mushroom Kingdom. Don't forget to bring your Power-Ups for an extra boost!

Important Information

+ Climate

The Mushroom Kingdom enjoys mild and pleasant weather year-round. However, travelers should be prepared for unexpected encounters with Koopa Troopas, Goombas, and other creatures.

+ Transportation

Transportation in the Mushroom Kingdom is as varied as its landscapes. From warp pipes to flying carpets, you'll find plenty of ways to get around. Just watch out for banana peels!
